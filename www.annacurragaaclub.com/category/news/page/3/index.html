Club News

Posted on 04/02/2015 by Kevin Stapleton
Comments

The club are back training in the last few weeks. Numbers are good and as always any member who wants to train, text Kevin Stapleton 0861797875 to be added to the text list. New members are also welcome.

The club are holding a mouse race night in the Saltee on the 28th of March. Live mice will be used and we would like to note that no mice will be harmed or injured in the event. Players and committee members will have race cards over the coming weeks and mice cost €5. Keep an eye out for more details.

Last man standing competition is starting this weekend. Full details are on our Facebook page and results will be updated weekly. Contact Nevan O’Sullivan or Kevin Stapleton for details.

Finally, there is lots of talk about our Facebook page lately. Great additions of player profiles going onto the page every few days and maybe a few Annacurra “legends” will also be contributing. Follow the link to see what’s in there so far. https://www.facebook.com/media/set/?set=a.10152612119930756.1073741828.210853230755&type=1

*The term legend is loosely describing players that retired a few years ago and not the man in the image. (Image used to portray the best dressed title he keeps appearing in)
