Playing as “Annacurra”, we have our own under 6, under 8, under 10 and under 12 teams.

Juvenile Fixtures

  • 2014 Under 12 Football Fixtures

The Annacurra club fields the following underage teams independently of our own:

  • Under-8, training is every Wednesday from 6.30pm to 7.30pm
  • Under-10, training is every Wednesday from 6.30pm to 7.30pm
  • Under-12, training every Thursday night from 6.30pm to 7.30pm

They are part of our main club and central to what we do as a GAA club and in the community.

  • Childrens Officer:                   Brian Moran  (087) 2355748
  • Coaching Co-ordinator            Declan Byrne (087) 7839872

At under 14, under 16 & minor level we join up with Aughrim to form the “Derry Gaels”.
