Fitness – Walking/ Running Group

Posted on 02/06/2014 by Sean McGrath
Comments

As part of our Healthy Club Project Annacurra GAA Club have arranged for an Athletics Ireland registered coach to come down to Annacurra for 5-6 weeks to help set up an adult fitness group  to get people running or walking more.

This is for anybody who wants to get a bit fitter or healthier and we will be catering for all levels, (couch potato to marathon runners), so everybody is welcome to come along regardless of fitness level’s or club membership.

The events will be taking place on a Thursday night at 8pm at the field till probably the 26th of June.

Looking forward to seeing you all there.

Fight Night

Posted on 23/02/2014 by Sean McGrath
Comments

The Annacurra GAA versus Tinahely GAA fight night was held in the Arklow Bay Hotel on Saturday the 22nd of February 2014 and was a fantastic success with some much needed money being raised for both clubs. Congratulations to Tinahely who won out on a score of 7 bought’s to 5 with one drawn bought.

We would like to thank all the fighters who put in so much hard work in preparation over the last several weeks and who did themselves and their respective parishes proud by donning gloves and stepping into the ring. E​veryone of them acquitted themselves fantastically and were a credit to their clubs and communities.

The Annacurra fighters were 

  • Brendan Nolan
  • Martin Darcy
  • Colm Meagher
  • Liam Moules
  • Alan Horan
  • Ciaran Byrne
  • Martin O’Brien
  • Eugen Clune
  • Sean O’Neill
  • Marcus Graham
  • James Walsh
  • Mick O’Brien
  • Chris Keogh

Commiserations to Darren Thompson and Leigh Horan who had trained for the event but were unable to take part because of injury and unavailability of an opponent respectively.

Also, great credit and thanks is due to Barry O’Toole and his team who trained both sides and helped to organise the event. Much credit also belongs to the players from both clubs who have taken in on themselves to organise this event and it is because of their efforts that it was such a success.

We would like to acknowledge in particular Mick O’Brien and Shane McAllister for their role in making sure that the event happened and went so well. We would also like to thank the countless other’s who put in effort to make it such a success.

If anyone has any outstanding ticket or sponsorship money can they please make sure to get it in as soon as possible.
